Mobile-First Design for Web Games

Best practices to ensure Endless Runner is smooth, intuitive, and enjoyable on any screen size.

Published 2025-08-22

Starting with mobile in mind guarantees a seamless experience across phones, tablets, and desktops, keeping controls natural and performance fluid.

Optimized Thumb Zones

Position interactive buttons and gesture areas where thumbs naturally rest to minimize strain and improve reaction times.

High-Performance Targets

Maintain under 12 ms per frame to consistently hit 60 fps, ensuring smooth drawing strokes and responsive gameplay.

Immediate Touch Feedback

Use visual cues or subtle sound effects to give players instant confirmation that taps, swipes, and drags have been registered.

Scalable Layouts

Design flexible UI elements that adjust to various screen sizes and orientations without breaking the user experience.
