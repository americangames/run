Sound & Feedback That Feel Good

Subtle audio and visual cues make every interaction satisfying and fun.

Published 2025-08-22

Crisp sound effects paired with gentle visual feedback transform routine actions into delightful moments of play.

Micro Sounds

Use short, precise audio cues to ensure instant response and keep interactions lively without overwhelming the player.

Visual Highlights

Quick flashes, subtle pulses, or slight shakes celebrate successes and actions without distracting from gameplay.

Respect Volume Settings

Always provide a mute option and respect the device’s volume settings to maintain comfort and control for players.
